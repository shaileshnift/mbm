Our Vision

The school is known for excellent discipline in this age of disillusionment and disenchantment where there is more decadence prevailing all around. Behavioural growth and changes are monitored intensively. Experienced and competent faculty are for academic excellence and all round development of students’ personality. Facility exists for career guidance and special training program for IIT, Medical, etc.

Our vision is to provide a happy, caring and stimulating environment where children will recognize and achieve their fullest potential, so that they can make their best contribution to society. Saint R. L. inter college is a place where everybody is welcomed and you are not alone. We work together for the betterment of society. We respect and care for everyone and everything around us. MBM sets high standards of learning and celebrates the achievements of each child. MBM is a place where everyone is different and has importance.

Our vision is that children leave school with:

1.&nbsp A set of moral values -- honesty, integrity and good judgement.
2.&nbsp A complement of basic skills -- linguistic, mathematical, scientific, artistic, physical and social.
3.&nbsp An enquiring and discriminating mind and a desire for knowledge.
4.&nbsp Strong self-esteem and high personal expectation.
5. Tolerance and respect for others.

We value the partnership which exists between school, parents and community and the part it plays in realizing this vision.

Our school will challenge children of all abilities to achieve excellence in a wide range of academic, cultural and sporting activities. It will equip children for the demands and opportunities of the
twenty-first century by offering a differentiated, effective and rigorous curriculum as an entitlement to all. A professional and highly motivated staff, in partnership with parents, will encourage each child to achieve his full potential. In a disciplined and caring environment, based on mutual respect, each child will be valued as an individual in his/her own right and his/her moral development encouraged.
