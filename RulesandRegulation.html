Rules And Regulation
Rules And Regulation
  1. Regular attendance is an important condition for successful working. A prior written application for leave must be submitted well in advance.
  2. All requests regarding absence or late-coming should be made in the school.
  3. Home work in all the subjects is given every day in all the classes. Guardians are requested to see that the child completes the home work regularly and must sign the home work diary every day.
  4. Every student must be present on the reopening day after each vacation.
  5. No student will be allowed to leave the school premises during school hours.
  6. Students are not allowed to carry mobiles or tablets in the school premises.
  7. If a student remains absent in any test/examination, he/she will not be permitted for re-examination.
  8. To bring about co-ordination between the parents and teachers, regular Parent-Teacher Meetings are organized.
  9. Parents are requested to inform the school in case there is any change of their address or phone number.
  10. Students must be punctual in reaching school. Late comers will not be allowed to enter the class without the Principal’s permission.
  11. Students not in proper uniform will not be allowed to enter the class.
  12. Parents and other persons are not allowed to meet their children or teachers during school hours.
  13. The Principal has the authority to rusticate any student whose conduct is not found good.
  14. Students are expected to take part in sports, debates, art competition, collage competition, quiz, writing competition, science and craft exhibition, outdoor activities and other competitions in extra-curricular activities by the school from time to time.
